The Radius Stanmore

When 25 years of development heritage comes together to build the first luxury apartment block in the centre of Stanmore, the right team needed to be behind the big expectations. In partnership with Richardson and Wrench, Hooch Creative became apart of this history making project.

Built on the iconic 75 year old Sailsburry service station the development was coined ‘The Radius’. It’s circular façade architecture, proximity to all amenities and Stanmore’s ability to be its own self sufficient suburb, The Radius had to be an all encompassing brand.We were delegated the task to deliver the strategy and execute all collateral that would sell the nine units at record breaking prices. At the first week of official release, five out of the nine apartments were sold.
