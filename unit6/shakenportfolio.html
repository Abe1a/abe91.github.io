The Shaken Japan

Creating a typographic substance that best interprets the theme of ‘it happened on this day’, with a delivery platform that demands the reader’s attention. It needs to address the norms of information architecture while actively working to evoke an emotional response from the reader or viewer.

The date chosen is Friday 11th of March 2011, the Japanese earthquake. The delivery platform for this topic is in the form of a newspaper, that visually has a connection to Japanese culture and style. The newspaper tells a story about the cause and effects of the event and how it has changed the Japanese community and the world forever. The event is broken down into small topics which informs the reader about what has happened and the ripple effects of this tragedy.
