Chill Bean Cafe

Design and brand a local cafe business. The cafe aims to attract new customers and stand out from its already existing competitors within it's local region.

Chill-Bean Cafe is branded to a highly sophisticated and boutique style. The cafe aims to bring life to the vintage and retro feel throughout its branding. A cafe where customers can come and not just grab a coffee or bite to eat but rather walk out with an experience to remember. An experience of a warm and family run business.
